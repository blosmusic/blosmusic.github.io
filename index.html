ABOUT

Ben Lamb O'Sullivan is a musician, tutor, and researcher.

The projects on this page represent the research and development of musical interfaces, tools, and instruments. They are been designed to help musicians within creative practice.

The work is ongoing as part of a PhD study concerned with DIY culture within music and technology and how it can be used to develop tools for musicians at all levels.

The study is being conducted at the Dundalk Institute of Technology, Ireland.
